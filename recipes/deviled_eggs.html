Deviled Eggs

Delicious and easy deviled eggs

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 2 TBSP mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp prepared mustard
  • 1 TBSP finely chopped onion
  • 1 TBSP finely chopped celery
  • 1/2 tsp salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch paprika, or to taste
  1. Peel hard-boiled eggs
  2. Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks. Set whites aside.
  3. Mash yolks with a fork in a small bowl. Stir in mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, onion, and celery. Mix well and season to taste.
  4. Stuff or pipe egg yolk mixture into egg whites.
  5. Sprinkle with paprika.
  6. Refrigerate eggs until serving.
